About GrowClean

GrowClean is a citizen-based public service complaint platform designed to help residents report and track local civic issues efficiently. It bridges the gap between citizens and municipal authorities through transparent complaint registration and resolution tracking.

Why Use GrowClean?

  • Report civic issues such as drainage, garbage, road damage, and water supply problems.
  • Track the status of complaints in real time.
  • Improve accountability and transparency in public services.
  • Use secure access with individual login credentials.

How GrowClean Works

  1. Register or log in using your credentials.
  2. File a complaint with category and details.
  3. Your complaint is routed to the concerned department.
  4. Track your complaint until it is resolved.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower citizens by giving them a voice in improving their local environment. GrowClean promotes cleaner and safer communities through technology-driven public participation.
